## Current Research Focus

The primary paper studies a weekly covered-call recovery framework across RR, POET, ONDS, and USAR. It combines Black-Scholes option modeling, put/call sentiment inputs, strike-selection rules, and assignment probability analysis.

The supplementary SPY report uses K-Means Clustering and a PyTorch LSTM to study market regimes that may provide context for future options-strategy research. It does not model option premiums or claim covered-call profitability.
