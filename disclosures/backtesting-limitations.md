# Backtesting Limitations

Backtests and modeled simulations are useful for research, but they do not guarantee future results.

## Common Limitations

- Historical data may not reflect future market behavior.
- TradingView results depend on platform settings, timeframe, symbol, data history, commissions, and slippage assumptions.
- Modeled option prices may differ from executable bid/ask quotes.
- Liquidity and fill quality can materially change real results.
- Assignment behavior for equity options can differ from simplified finish-above-strike probability models.
- Strategy performance can degrade when market regimes, volatility, spreads, or execution conditions change.

## Interpretation

Any performance metric in this repository should be read as research output under stated assumptions. Live trading decisions require current market data, risk controls, compliance review where applicable, and independent judgment.
